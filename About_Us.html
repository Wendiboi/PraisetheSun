All about us!

We as students want easier access to information and other students. We all sometimes need a little help. So a couple stupid and bored students decided that no one was going to make an application that would help check what exams are coming up, especially which holidays we all will soon be celebrating. Thus these stupid students got together and made CTU-Buddy, a website for all of us.

What is our vision?

CTU-Buddy aims for a great user experience. If you forgot what subject you are writing this week, check out the Timetable. You can't find that one assignment rubric you accidentally deleted, hop on over to the Share Resources tab. Just want to vent about how you suffered in the last exam, the discussions page is there for you. CTU-Buddy aims to be easy to use and accessible for all students here at CTU.

What is CTU Buddy?

CTU-Buddy is an online web-based application used by students. It provides a space for students to find assistance whenever they need it. CTU-Buddy has a multitude of pages all for the purpose of being there for the student of CTU. The Share Resources page can be used to upload or download files between students. The Discussion page can be used to reply to and answer threads for students. The contact us page can be used for finding ways to communicate with the team behind CTU-Buddy. The Timetable page can be used to check on upcoming exams, holidays and the classes the students have for that week.
